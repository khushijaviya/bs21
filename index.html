WHAT IS CPI?

CPI Means Cumulative Performance Index For 2 Years Course : If duration of course is of two years, the degree shall be given to students based upon CPI (Cumulative Performance Index) considering all the four semesters performance.

WHAT IS STPI?

Merit Mark for D2D Admissions of Degree Engineering for GTU Students is based on Semester Theory Performance Index (STPI). STPI is a GPA of Sem-5 & Sem-6 Subjects of only external theory exam (i.e. out of 70 marks). Theory ESE component of GTU result is used to calculate STPI.
